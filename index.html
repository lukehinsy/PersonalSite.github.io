About

Lucas Hinsenkamp
PhD — Analytic Consultant

I'm an analytic consultant with a PhD in social psychology and a habit of making complex things click. Over the past decade I've helped organizations — from scrappy research shops to large insurers — turn murky business questions into frameworks that actually get used. I like the messy middle: the part where the problem isn't fully defined yet and you have to earn the insight rather than inherit it.

These days I'm a strategic lead in analytics at Nationwide, working across consumer segmentation, sales optimization, and a handful of things that didn't have names when I started them. I care a lot about rigor, but I care just as much about making sure the work lands — with stakeholders, in decisions, in the world.
